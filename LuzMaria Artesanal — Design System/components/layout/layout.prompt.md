**SectionDivider**, **OrnateFrame**, **Eyebrow** — layout accents. Ornament is the exception, never the pattern.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Sobre a marca</Eyebrow>
<SectionDivider variant="node" />
<OrnateFrame><p>Feitas à mão, com fé e propósito…</p></OrnateFrame>
```

`SectionDivider` is a thin gold filete (`line` or `node` with a diamond) between blocks. `OrnateFrame` is the single baroque corner-bracket moldura — reserve it for the brand-story / artisanal highlight and never tile it. `Eyebrow` is the small-caps gold label above headings.
