**WhatsAppButton** — the brand's main conversion action; wraps `Button` with the WhatsApp glyph and a pre-filled, first-person message.

```jsx
<WhatsAppButton
  phone="5511999999999"
  message="Vim pela landing page e tenho interesse na Vela Nossa Senhora Aparecida. Pode me passar valores e prazo?"
>Quero fazer minha encomenda</WhatsAppButton>
```

Keep the message devotional and personal ("Vim pela landing page e tenho interesse…"). Use `primary` for the hero/checkout CTA; `secondary` inside product cards.
