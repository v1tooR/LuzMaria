**ProductCard** — a single devotional candle; dark surface with a fine gold border, centered chiaroscuro photo, ivory name + gold price, and an in-card WhatsApp CTA. Keep the inside clean — no ornamentation.

```jsx
<ProductCard
  name="Vela Nossa Senhora Aparecida"
  description="Peça delicada e devocional, ideal para oratórios e presentes."
  price="R$ 68,00"
  image="assets/img/candle-aparecida.jpg"
  phone="5512997777389"
  message="Vim pela landing page e tenho interesse na Vela Nossa Senhora Aparecida..."
/>
```

Hover lifts the card and brightens the border with a soft candle glow. Pass `badge` for a small gold corner label. Lay out in a 3-up grid (`g3`) on desktop, 1-up on mobile.
